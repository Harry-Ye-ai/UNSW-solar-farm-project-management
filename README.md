# UNSW Solar Farm Project Management Plan

Designed and planned a 20MW photovoltaic solar farm project, including budgeting, scheduling, risk management, and stakeholder coordination.

## Key Highlights
- Planned a 20MW solar farm (expandable to 30MW)
- Managed ~$25M budget with top-down & bottom-up estimation
- Designed full project lifecycle (WBS, Gantt chart, risk register)
- Reduced energy cost by ~20% and carbon emissions by ~30%

- Designed risk management framework and contingency plans
